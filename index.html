Kevin's Game Collection

Six browser-based games — pick your adventure

Super Mario Bros

Platformer Single-file

Run, jump, and stomp through a side-scrolling level inspired by the NES classic. Coins, Goombas, Koopa Troopas, power-ups, and a flagpole finish await.

Arrow keys / WASD · Space / Up to jump

Tetris

Puzzle Single-file

Classic block-stacking with ghost piece, next-piece preview, wall kicks, increasing speed, and procedural chiptune music. Touch support included.

Arrow keys · Space to hard drop · P to pause

Silent Thrill

3D Horror Three.js

A first-person survival horror experience in a foggy graveyard. Survive ghost patrols using only your flashlight. Features dynamic AI, health system, and immersive audio.

WASD · Mouse to look · F for flashlight

Theme Park

Tycoon Simulation

Build and manage your own theme park. Place paths, rides, shops, and facilities on a grid while guests wander, ride, eat, and rate your park. Economy, maintenance, and guest AI included.

Click to build · Right-click to remove · Space to pause

Whack-a-Mole

Arcade Single-file

Classic arcade action — moles pop up from 9 holes in a 30-second frenzy. Features combos, particle effects, floating scores, procedural sound effects, and high score tracking.

Click the moles · 30-second rounds

Zork

Text Adventure Interactive Fiction

A terminal-style interactive fiction adventure inspired by the Infocom classic. Explore 11 rooms, collect items, solve the world, and watch the auto-map grow — all with a CRT monitor aesthetic.

Type commands · n/s/e/w/u/d to move
